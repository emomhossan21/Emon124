About CyberGuard

CyberGuard is a premier cybersecurity training platform founded by Emon Hossan Miazi, dedicated to educating the next generation of ethical hackers.

Our mission is to bridge the skills gap in cybersecurity by providing hands-on training with real-world scenarios.

With over 10 years of industry experience, our instructors bring practical knowledge to help students develop in-demand skills.
